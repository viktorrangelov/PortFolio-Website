My Personality

My name is Viktor Rangelov and I am from Plovdiv Bulgaria. I am one of those people trying to get the best possible of their time, my daily schedule includes work, studying, sports, and hobby time. I really love playing video games and building lego sets. What I like best about legos is that you start with bags of parts, follow a specific plan, and end up with a finished project; it somehow relates to my life plan. As a member of society, I believe it is my responsibility that my actions do not harm the environment - I always try to separate trash, I rarely drive a car - preferring an electric scooter or a bike - and I am the person who always turns off the lights and the air conditioning in empty rooms around the office. If you get to know me, you'll notice that I am frequently the soul of the company, and there is a lot of laughter among my colleagues when I am around.

My Experience

After graduating from Plovdiv's English high school, I began my first job as a sales representative for a company that is Nintendo's exclusive distributor in Bulgaria. This position led me to Marketing, and I earned a Bachelor's degree in Marketing at the University of Plovdiv. My interest in computers led me to my current position as a Technical Support Engineer at SiteGround after graduation. I am currently employed and familiar with plenty of web technologies and still I am studying javascript at Softuni, with the goal of becoming a javascript master.
